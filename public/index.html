Some of Your Favorite Features

Based on our statistics, these are some of the most popular features of Utility Belt.

/image-to-gif

Convert any image to a gif with a simple command. Great for sharing memes and reactions with your friends.

/speech-bubble

Add a speech bubble to any image. The fastest way to make any image into a meme.

/video-to-gif

Command for taking a video link and converting it to a gif you can save and share.

/download

Download media from dozens of websites with a simple command. Includes YouTube, SoundCloud, TikTok and more! Choose between audio or video downloads with a customizable quality selector.

/imagine

AI generate an image with SDXL! Contains plenty of customizable options including Img2Img, Strength and Seed.

/notes

Take notes in a simple and easy to use manor. All right from the comfort of your discord server.
